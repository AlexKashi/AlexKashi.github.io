Current Work

I am currently a researcher at the Stanford Genome Technology Center. My main objective is to help find new diagnostics methods for Chronic Fatigue Syndrom and discover potential treatments. To do this we employ the use of novel bio sensors fabricated at the nano scale. When used properly these bio senors may be able to differentiate controls from people with CFS. Unfortionatly I can not go into too much detail because the results have not been published, please keep an eye out for the publication in the coming months.
