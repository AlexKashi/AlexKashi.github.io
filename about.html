GTM Supercar

As of October 2019, my dad and I have completed over a decade long project of building a GTM Supercar. The car is built on the V-8 LS376/525 engine, hitting a maximum horsepower of 525 at 6200 RPM. With the current gear ratio of the transaxle, the vehicle can reach a top speed of over 200 MPH!

Innovations for Custom Cars

We engineered many custom technologies to showcase in this one of a kind car. We simplified the wiring in cars by developing smart programmable modular controllers that can be used to control almost any aspect of the vehicle with custom behavior. We also created a custom gauge cluster that communicates with the car via OBD-II. I helped develop these skins on Android using QT. We are currently looking into a fully embedded approach running on an Nvidia Tegra processor to remove the android computer and substantially increase computational power.
